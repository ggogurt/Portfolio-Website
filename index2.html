This page is about noticing the same number everywhere, sometimes it's referred to as "priming" yourself. For me I notice 29 EVERYWHERE. I was born on the 29th and I guess I somehow trained myself to only see that number. It first started when my sibling saw 23 all the time (they were born on the 23rd). Obviously I wanted my own number so I started looking for any same number for the next 2 days. I looked at microwave times, addresses, anything with the time basically. I eventually gave up after nothing stuck and of course 29 started popping up all over the place. It was a building next to Jones Hall (right next to a 23 also), It was on train markers, the time, construction graffiti on the ground, it was fucking everywhere.

During my summer this year, I kept seeing an angel number. 444 Kept following me around, especially when I was at work. I was working in a 80 degree fahrenheit factory that was constantly in the 80 - 90 decibel range for 12 hours a day building server racks. All of the racks needed labels, and every weekly shift I worked I somehow kept getting 444. Not only that, but I would check the time to see when it was 6pm to leave and at least once a week it would be 4:44. Anyways all of this is to commentate on repeating numbers and how common it can be.

I wanted this page to be space because I feel like the commonality of angel numbers and birthday numbers are somehow related to the stars or something greater. I personally think chance is something that belongs not on Earth.

The rest of this website past the space section is just a little area for things I like or find funny.
